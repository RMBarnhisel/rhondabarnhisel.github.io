06 — Point of View

How I think about the work.

Creative has to earn its keep.

The best creative direction isn't the prettiest — it's the one tied to a business priority. I've spent my career aligning the two, so the work moves a number, not just a mood board.

Under budget is a creative skill.

Delivering 12 episodes a season on time and on budget isn't a constraint on creativity — it's proof of it. Constraints are where the real producing happens.

Live is the truest test.

When the broadcast is live into millions of homes and the CEO is on, there are no second takes. That pressure taught me clarity: know the one thing the audience must walk away with.

Brand is a story people repeat.

A brand isn't a logo or a palette — it's the story your audience tells about you when you're not in the room. My job is to make that story clear, true, and worth repeating.
